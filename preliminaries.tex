\large{\textbf{INSTRUCTIONS}}

\normalfont{
\begin{enumerate}
\item DO NOT OPEN THIS BOOKLET UNTIL THE TIMER STARTS.
\item This is a twenty-five question multiple choice test. Each question is followed by answers marked A, B, C, D and E. Only one of these is correct.
\item Mark your answer clearly, edits in submission will not be accepted.
\item SCORING: You will receive 6 points for each correct answer, 1.5 points for each problem left unanswered, and 0 points for each incorrect answer.
\item No aids are permitted other than scratch paper, graph paper, rulers, compass, protractors, and erasers. No calculators, smartwatches, or computing devices are allowed. No problems on the test will require the use of a calculator.
\item Figures are not necessarily drawn to scale.
\item Before beginning the test, your proctor will ask you to record certain information on the answer form.
\item When your proctor gives the signal, begin working on the problems. You will have 75 minutes to complete the test.
\item When you finish the exam, sign your name in the space provided on the Answer Form.
\end{enumerate}
